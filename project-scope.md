# Project Scope

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Executive KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Inventory Value
* Average Lead Time
* Gross Margin %
* Forecast Accuracy %

### 2. Demand Analysis

* Units Sold by Month
* Units Sold by Region
* Top 10 SKUs by Volume

### 3. Inventory Analysis

* Inventory Level by Warehouse
* Inventory Level by SKU
* Inventory Value by Warehouse

### 4. Supplier Performance

* Average Lead Time by Supplier
* Supplier Ranking

### 5. Inventory Risk Model

Risk Score Components:

* Inventory Position
* Demand Pressure
* Lead Time Risk
* Forecast Error

## Project Goal

Build an Excel-based Supply Chain Performance Dashboard that analyzes inventory performance, supplier effectiveness, demand trends, and operational risk within a manufacturing supply chain environment.
