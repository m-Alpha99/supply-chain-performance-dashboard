Supply Chain Performance Dashboard
Overview

This project analyzes over 90,000 supply chain transactions to evaluate inventory performance, demand trends, supplier responsiveness, and operational efficiency within a manufacturing and industrial supply chain environment.

The goal is to develop an Excel-based dashboard that transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights for inventory optimization and supply chain decision-making.

Business Problem

Manufacturing organizations must balance inventory availability with inventory costs while maintaining service levels and supporting customer demand.

Common challenges include:

Excess inventory carrying costs
Demand variability
Supplier lead time fluctuations
Replenishment planning inefficiencies
Stockout risk

This project uses data analytics techniques to identify these challenges and recommend data-driven improvements.

Objectives
Analyze inventory performance across warehouses
Evaluate supplier lead times and responsiveness
Measure demand variability and forecast accuracy
Identify inventory optimization opportunities
Develop executive-level supply chain KPIs
Build an interactive Excel dashboard
